Got questions?

Everything you need to know

Common questions about FITNESS CLUB membership and programs.

What experience level is required to join?

+

We welcome fitness enthusiasts of all levels. Our programs range from beginner-friendly sessions to advanced elite circuits.

Do I need to sign up for specific sessions?

+

Yes. Each session is scheduled in advance, and members reserve a spot through our weekly schedule.

Are sessions guided or self-directed?

+

Most sessions are guided by experienced coaches. You can also train independently using our program guides.

What should I bring to a training session?

+

Proper training shoes, water bottle, and workout-appropriate clothing. We handle everything else.

Can I try a session before becoming a member?

+

Yes. We offer one complimentary trial session so you can experience the atmosphere before committing.
