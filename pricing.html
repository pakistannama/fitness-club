Got questions?

Everything you need to know

Common questions about our membership plans.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

+

Yes. All paid memberships can be cancelled at any time with no cancellation fees. Your membership remains active until the end of the billing period.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

+

Absolutely. You can switch between plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately and downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is the trial session really free?

+

Yes, completely free. One trial session per person, available to non-members only. No card required to book a trial session.

Are there any hidden fees?

+

No hidden fees whatsoever. The monthly price shown is the only amount you will be charged. No enrollment fees, no equipment fees.

What is included in the Elite plan?

+

Elite includes unlimited sessions, all program access, 1-on-1 coach sessions, weekly tracking, nutrition guidance, recovery planning, and competition prep support.
